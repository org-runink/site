Meet Your New Team

Don't Managed Data. Manage Outcomes.

Dashboards don't pay the bills. Actions do. Runink isn't a tool for looking at charts. It's a set of engines that do the work for you.

1. Rules Studio

Teach It Once.

You know how to run your business. The problem is, you can't be everywhere at once. Rules Studio lets you teach the system your "Best Practices" one time, and it remembers them forever.

Teach it to recover revenue: "If a shipment sits for 7 days, chase the carrier."
Teach it to block risk: "If a duplicate order comes in, stop it."

  • ✓ Performance: See how much money your rules saved you.
  • ✓ Detail View: Check exactly why a rule fired.
  • ✓ Deploy: Push new rules instantly.
2. Decision Cockpit

Work Gets Done.

This isn't a chat bot. It's a command center. Your digital workers sit here, waiting for your approval to take action.

The "Action Deck" shows you the work that's ready to go. A PO that needs signing. A refund claim ready to file. You review the work, hit [Approve], and they go do it.

  • ✓ Ordering Agent: "I found the best price. Ready to order?"
  • ✓ Claims Agent: "I found 14 lost packages. File for refund?"
  • ✓ Simulation: "If we wait, we lose $8.5k in sales."
3. AI Posture Center

Sleep At Night.

You can't trust automation if it breaks every time something changes. The Readiness Scoreboard gives you peace of mind.

It checks your infrastructure on demand. If a connection gets slow or data looks weird, it flags it immediately. You can verify system health whenever you need to ensure stability.

  • ✓ Incident Timeline: See what happened and why.
  • ✓ Scoreboard: A simple grade for your system's health.
  • ✓ Auto-Fix: It suggests fixes for technical problems.
4. Hypothesis Lab

Test The Future.

Making big changes is scary. Runink removes the fear by letting you simulate the future.

"If we raise prices 5%, do we lose customers?" The AI checks your history and tells you the likely outcome. Know the result before you make the move.

  • ✓ See The Impact: "Margin up 4%, Volume down 8%."
  • ✓ Confidence Meter: Know how sure the AI is.
  • ✓ Make It Real: Turn a good idea into a rule instantly.

Trust Architecture

You're always in control.

1. Shadow Mode

Test safely. Runink watches your data and simulates actions without doing them. See exactly what the agents would have done (e.g., "I would have filed this claim") before you give them the keys.

2. Graduated Autonomy

You set the leash. Start with "Junior Analyst Mode" (Agent drafts, you approve). Graduate to "Co-Pilot" (Agent handles small tasks). Finally, unlock "Full Autonomy" only when you're ready.
