Usage-based billing — pay only for what you analyze

Runink charges only for what you actually process. Every query scanned, data connection added, and GB analyzed counts toward your usage credits.

1

Connect your data sources

Each source (Snowflake, BigQuery, Databricks, etc.) counts as a connection. The first 3 are included; extras are billed monthly.

2

We monitor and analyze your queries

Each query inspected or optimized by Runink counts toward your usage limit. Dashboards show your monthly and annual usage.

3

Pay only for overages

If you exceed your plan’s limits, small overage rates apply for queries, GB analyzed, or connections. Annual plans include pooled usage across all teams.
