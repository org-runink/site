Billing Logic. No Surprises.

Low barrier to entry. Revenue scales with your actual infrastructure usage.

Infrastructure is pass-through at cost.
• 1 DU = 1GB of data processed = $0.01
• 1 CU = 60 seconds of compute time = $0.01

You pay your base tier ($20 or $80/mo) for platform features. DU/CU scales with actual usage—no markup.
Core ($20/mo): All the visibility and testing.
