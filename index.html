The Automated Returns Triage Loop

Circular Economy & Value Recovery

The Operational Problem

Manual returns processing causes slow refund cycles, massive warehouse congestion, and major financial leakages as high-value, refurbishable products sit idle or are mistakenly sent to landfills.
